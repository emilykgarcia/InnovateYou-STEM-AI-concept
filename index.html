Who We Are

InnovateYou STEM is a nonprofit dedicated to providing girls — especially those from underserved communities — with the resources, support, and inspiration they need to thrive in science, technology, engineering, and math.

We believe the future of STEM should be as diverse, creative, and brilliant as the minds we uplift.

What We Do

  • STEM Workshops: Hands-on learning sessions for elementary and middle school girls.
  • Mentorship: One-on-one and group mentoring with women in STEM fields.
  • Podcast: Inspiring conversations with female scientists, engineers, and innovators.
  • STEM Spotlight: Weekly features highlighting underrepresented women making waves in STEM.
  • Tutoring: Free support for students in math, coding, and science.
  • Camps: Themed summer and weekend camps to spark curiosity and creativity.
